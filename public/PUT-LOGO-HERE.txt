Save the company logo image in THIS folder named exactly:

    logo.png

(Use the PropVerse.ai logo PNG you shared.)

Once it's here as nk-frontend/public/logo.png it will automatically appear
in the top header and on the login screen. No code change or restart needed —
just refresh the browser. If the file is missing, the app simply shows the
text title instead (no error).

A .jpg works too, but then rename the references in src/App.jsx from
"/logo.png" to "/logo.jpg" (two places: header and login).
